About Us

Empowering Her Voice. Protecting Her Future.

The Association of Female Comrades (AFC) is a registered non-governmental organization dedicated to empowering women and girls across Nigeria. We champion gender equality, leadership development, and community protection through advocacy, outreach, education, and anti-violence interventions.

Mission

To empower and uplift women and girls through advocacy, education, leadership training, and community-driven initiatives.

Vision

A world where every girl and woman is safe, valued, and empowered to reach her full potential.

Core Values

  • Integrity
  • Inclusivity
  • Empowerment
  • Courage
  • Service to Humanity
  • Collaboration
